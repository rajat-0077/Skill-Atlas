OUR MISSION

Making Learning Clear and Accessible for Everyone

Skill Atlas is a free, community-driven platform designed to eliminate confusion and help you navigate the overwhelming sea of online resources.

What is Skill Atlas?

Whether you are preparing for a competitive exam like JEE or UPSC, or trying to pick up a new tech or creative skill, knowing where to start is often the hardest part. Skill Atlas provides curated, step-by-step visual roadmaps that break down complex subjects into digestible modules.

100% Free & Community Driven

Education should not be locked behind a paywall. Skill Atlas is completely free to use. Our roadmaps, notes, and community discussions are powered by students, educators, and professionals who volunteer their time to share their knowledge.

Open Source

Skill Atlas believes in transparency. The entire platform is open source on GitHub. We welcome developers to help us build new features and improve the user experience.
